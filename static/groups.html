Groups

Info for the different groups we run

Groups Info

  • Service of Worship

    Sunday 10:30am / *11:00am | at the Church Hall, Main Entrance

    On Sunday we meet in the church building, (5 Church St, Clinton 9534, New Zealand) we sing and a sermon is given

    *Our services from August to October run at 11:00am with bring and share lunch afterwards

  • Mini Movers

    Wednesday (During School Terms) | 9:00am | at Clarke Hall

    All mums with children are welcome to join us for a complimentary music session and morning tea

  • Mum's Bible Study

    Wednesday 10:30am | at Clarke Hall

    The Mum's Bible study is a group run every week on a Wednesday directly after Mini Movers, We go through the Bible sometimes using external resources such as the Alpha Course to provide a structure. It is run by some of the Mums in the church for learning about God and His word together with other Mums

  • Prayer Meeting

    Wednesday 7:30pm | at Clarke Hall

    Every week we run the Prayer Meeting to bring to God anything and everything that has been going on over the week and ask for His guidance, direction and help; to give thanks and praise for all He has done for us.

    Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God. And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus.
    — Philippians 4:6-7 NIV

  • Bible Study

    Thursday | 10:00am | at Clarke Hall

    on Thursday mornings we run a Bible Study Group, this is meant for everyone who wants to come along and is able to. This group is for people to get together and learn about God and His word over a cup of coffee and some morning tea

    All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness, so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work.
    — 2 Timothy 3:16-17 NIV

  • Men's Group

    Thursday 7:30pm | at Clarke Hall

    Men's Group is a group overseen by Peter Wilson, it is a group for men to come along and have an open discussion with each other, then afterwards to enjoy food and fellowship together.

  • Youth Group

    Friday (During School Term) | 7:00pm - 8:45pm | at Clarke Hall

    Join us for awesome games, crazy adventures and sweet food every Friday night! Free Admission and supper provided. Ages: 11-16
